The Art Of Living Dallas Center

About

The Art of Living Foundation, known worldwide under the name Art of Living, is a charitable organization and an NGO dedicated to creating a stress-free, violence-free society. Art of Living spreads peace across communities through diverse humanitarian projects, including disaster relief, sustainable rural development, empowerment of women, education for all and environmental sustainability. Since 1981, we have been teaching breathing and meditation techniques around the globe. Present in 155 countries, the Foundation has already reached over 370 million individuals. Art of Living centres can be found in most of the major cities including the one thats to be newly built here in our own Dallas city.

Vision

Art of Living Dallas Center outlines a Center of Peace, Knowledge, and Wisdom that will empower and strengthen our local community. It reflects the core values of Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar.
We Serve Society by Strengthening the Individual. "The Art of Living is more of a principle, a philosophy of living life to its fullest. It is more a movement than an organization. Its core value is to find peace within oneself and to unite people in our society – of different cultures, traditions, religions, nationalities; and thus reminding us all that we have one goal to uplift human life everywhere."

Mission

To have a home , which will bring miracles in the lives of millions of people; a home where - tears turn into smiles, desperation turns into hope, dullness turns into creativity and hatred turns into love. - Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar

Location

A 10-acre land in Lewisville off Hwy 121 has been located for the New Center. Property Address: 1632 Bunker Hill Lane, Lewisville, TX 75056.

Site Selection:

Dallas Fort Worth (DFW) Metropolis is in the 4th largest city with 13 counties and is the Central hub of North America. 2 Million Indian diasporas in DFW, growing by the day (400 new residents added to Dallas every day). Art of Living DFW Chapter is the 3rd fastest growing center in US (next to SFO, NJ). Proposed site is well connected to all major highways and close to international airports (less than 20 mins drive). The project site is centered on the DFW Metroplex map. Central hub for all Art of Living activities in the DFW area – courses, yoga camps, satsang, knowledge sessions, meditations. Based on its close proximity to the major cities for example Irving, Frisco etc. the location was selected. 15 active weekly Sudarshan Kriya locations - an average of 200+ people attending.
